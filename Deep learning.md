# Deep learning

## Summary

A subset of ML: $\text{AI} \supset \text{ML} \supset \text{deep learning}$. Powerful and fast-moving, but narrower than the full ML toolkit. Neural nets are trained via **backpropagation / gradient descent**. CS229 covers the basics of training a neural net; **CS230** is the dedicated deep-learning course.
