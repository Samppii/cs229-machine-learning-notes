# Clustering

## Summary

An [[Unsupervised learning]] method that groups unlabeled data into clusters; the classic algorithm is **K-means**. Examples: Google News grouping same-story articles, genetic microarray data, market segmentation, communities in social networks, organizing compute clusters, and grouping galaxies.
