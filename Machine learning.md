# Machine learning

## Summary

**Arthur Samuel:** the field of study that gives computers the ability to learn *without being explicitly programmed*. Samuel's checkers program learned by playing itself thousands of times, and eventually became better at checkers than Samuel himself.

The more rigorous framing is the [[Well-posed learning problem]] (Mitchell). ML is a subfield of AI; its own subfields previewed in this course are [[Supervised learning]], [[Learning theory]], [[Deep learning]], [[Unsupervised learning]], and [[Reinforcement learning]].
